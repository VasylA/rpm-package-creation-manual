Features:

• Logic & mixed signal circuits;

• Highly versatile macro cells;

• Read back protection (Read lock);

• 1.8V (±5%) to 5V (±10%) supply;

• 1.8V (±5%) to 3.3V (±10%) supply (when using the 2 MHz internal OSC);

• Operating temperature range -40 °C to 85 °C;

• 2 A P-FET Power Switch;

• Package:

- 1.6 × 2.5 × 0.55 mm STQFN 14L package;

- Pb-Free / Halogen-free / RoHS compliant;


Applications:

• Power sequencing with complex analog control;

• Power plane component size reduction project;

• LED driver;

• Haptic motor driver;

• System RESET with Power Switch;


The GreenPAK3 SLG46118 is Silego’s third generation GreenPAK product and is a one-time programmable Mixed signal array coming in a small 1.6 mm x 1.6mm STQFN-12 package.

The GreenPAK operates at a 1.8V to 5V voltage range and provides a small, low power component for commonly used Mixed-signal functions.

Users create their circuit design by programming Non-Volatile Memory (NVM) to configure interconnect logic, I/O PINs and macrocells of the SLG46118. This highly versatile device allows a wide variety of Mixed-signal functions to be designed within a single very small, low power integrated circuit.

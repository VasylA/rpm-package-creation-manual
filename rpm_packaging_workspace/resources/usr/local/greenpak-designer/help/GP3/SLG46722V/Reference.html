Features:

• Logic & mixed signal circuits;

• Highly versatile macro cells;

• Read back protection (Read Lock);

• 1.8 V (±5%) to 5 V (±10%) supply;

• Operating temperature range: -40°C to 85°C;

Applications:

• Personal computers and servers;

• Consumer electronics;

• Data communications equipment;

• Handheld and portable electronics;


The GreenPAK3 SLG46722 is Silego’s third generation GreenPAK product and is a one-time programmable Mixed signal array coming in a small 2 mm x 3mm TDFN-20 package.

The GreenPAK3 operates at a 1.8V to 5V voltage range and provides a small, low power component for commonly used Mixed-signal functions.

Users create their circuit design by programming a one time Non-Volatile Memory (NVM) to configure interconnect logic, I/O PINs and macrocells of the SLG46722. This highly versatile device allows a wide variety of mixed-signal functions to be designed within a very small, low power single integrated circuit.

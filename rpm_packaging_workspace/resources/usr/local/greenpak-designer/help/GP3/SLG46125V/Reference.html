Features:

• Logic & mixed signal circuits;

• Highly versatile macro cells;

• Read back protection (Read lock);

• 1.8V (±5%) to 5V (±10%) supply;

• Operating temperature range -40 °C to 85 °C;

• Dual Soft-Start 45 mΩ, 2A P-FET Power Switches;

• Package:

- 1.6 × 2.0 × 0.55 mm MSTQFN 16L package;

- Pb-Free / Halogen-free / RoHS compliant;


Applications:

• Power sequencing with complex analog control;

• Power plane component size reduction project;

• LED driver;

• Haptic motor driver;

• System RESET with Power Switch;


The SLG46125 GreenPAK3 combines the functionality and versatility of a GreenPAK Programmable Mixed-signal Matrix with the capabilities of Silego’s CuFET technology. Capable of integrating a number common discrete ICs and passive components into a single device, the GreenPAK3 family’s SLG46125 enables high power switching with dual soft-start 2 A P-Ch MOSFETs with slew rate control
